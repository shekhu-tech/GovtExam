Rural Banking

RRBs provide credit for agriculture, trade, and commerce in rural areas. Working here directly contributes to India's rural development.

Various Posts

Recruitment for Group 'A' Officers (Scale-I, II, III) and Group 'B' Office Assistants (Multipurpose).

Local Posting

Posting is usually within the state you opted for. Keeps you closer to home. Local language knowledge is mandatory.

Career Growth

Stable career with promotion opportunities. Move from Assistant to Officer, or Scale-I to Senior Management.

Prelims (Qualifying)

  • For: Office Asst. & Scale-I only.
  • Questions: 80 (40 Reasoning, 40 Quant).
  • Marks: 80.
  • Time: 45 Minutes (Composite).

Mains / Single Exam

  • For: All Posts (Stage 2 for Asst/Scale-I).
  • Subjects: Reasoning, Quant, English/Hindi, Computer, GA.
  • Questions: 200.
  • Marks: 200.
  • Time: 2 Hours.

Interview

Final stage for Officer Cadre (Scale I, II, III). 100 Marks. No interview for Office Assistants.

Detailed Eligibility

Office Assistant: 18-28 Yrs. Any Degree. No Exp.
Scale-I: 18-30 Yrs. Any Degree (Agri pref). No Exp.
Scale-II: 21-32 Yrs. Degree + 2 Yrs Exp.
Scale-III: 21-40 Yrs. Degree + 5 Yrs Exp.
