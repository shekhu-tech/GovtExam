Tier-I CBT Exam

This Computer Based Test (CBT) is a screening test. The marks from this exam are used for shortlisting candidates for the next stage of the selection process. The syllabus includes General Awareness, Reasoning, English, and Quantitative Aptitude.

Tier-II Skill / Physical Fitness Test

Candidates who clear the Tier-I exam are called for a Tier-II test, which is a skill or physical fitness test depending on the post. This stage is qualifying in nature and does not affect the final merit list, which is prepared based on Tier-I marks.

Document Verification & Medical Check

After the final merit list is published based on Tier-I marks and qualifying in Tier-II, selected candidates undergo a thorough document verification to confirm their eligibility. This is followed by a medical examination to ensure they are fit for the role.
