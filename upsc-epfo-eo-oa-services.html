Enforcement Officer (EO)

Enforcement Officers primarily do field work. They ensure that establishments are complying with the Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952.

Work: Conducting inspections, inquiries, settling claims, and ensuring compliance.

Accounts Officer (AO)

Accounts Officers primarily do office work. They are responsible for maintaining accounts, managing cash flow, and preparing financial statements for the EPFO.

Work: Account maintenance, auditing, and managing financial statements.
