1. What is Teacher Recruitment? ü§î

Every state has its own Teacher Recruitment Board that recruits teachers for various government schools. The recruitment exam is usually conducted through eligibility tests like **TET (Teacher Eligibility Test)**, **CTET** or **REET**. Its main job is to select the most suitable candidates for **Primary Teacher (PRT), Trained Graduate Teacher (TGT), Post Graduate Teacher (PGT)** and other **many important posts**, so they can strengthen the education system.

12. Career Roadmap üöÄ

Teachers have a respectful career with opportunities for promotion to Headmaster/Principal.

Teaching Posts

School Level

1. Primary Teacher (PRT)

Teaching classes 1 to 5. Focus on foundational literacy and numeracy.

2. Trained Graduate Teacher (TGT)

Teaching classes 6 to 8. Subject specific teaching.

3. Post Graduate Teacher (PGT)

Teaching classes 9 to 12. Advanced subject knowledge required.

4. Shiksha Mitra / Asst Teacher

Assisting main teachers in primary schools.

Higher Secondary

5. Lecturer

Teaching senior secondary classes (11th-12th). Preparing students for college.
