3. Who can apply? (Eligibility by Entry) ‚úÖ

Candidates must meet the specific qualifications for their desired entry. Physical standards also apply as per Army regulations.

‚öïÔ∏è Army Medical Corps (AMC) - Officer

Education: MBBS with post-graduate degree/diploma (MD/MS/MCH/DNB/DLO etc.). Must have a permanent registration from a State Medical Council.
Age: Below 45 years.

üë©‚Äç‚öïÔ∏è Military Nursing Service (MNS) - Officer

Education: B.Sc (Nursing) / M.Sc (Nursing). Must be a registered Nurse and Midwife. For female candidates only.
Age: Typically between 21 and 35 years.

üèÖ Sports Quota Recruitment - Havildar

Education: Minimum Matriculation.
Sports Achievement: Outstanding sportspersons who have won medals at National/International levels.
Age: 17.5 to 25 years.

üõ°Ô∏è Defence Security Corps (DSC) - Soldier

Eligibility: For Ex-Servicemen of the Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force only.
Character: Must have an "Exemplary" or "Very Good" character assessment in their previous service.
Age: Varies by rank (e.g., up to 48 years for Sepoy).

5. Pay and Allowances üí∞

Pay and allowances are as per the rank and pay level in the Defence Pay Matrix, ensuring a secure and dignified career.

1. Officer Pay (AMC/MNS)

Officers are commissioned as Captains or Lieutenants and receive pay at Level 10B/10 of the pay matrix.

  • Includes Basic Pay, Military Service Pay (MSP), and Non-Practicing Allowance (for AMC).
  • Attractive salary with regular increments and promotions.

2. Other Ranks Pay (Sports/DSC)

Personnel are enrolled as Havildars or Sepoys and receive pay as per their rank.

  • All personnel are entitled to Dearness Allowance, HRA, Transport Allowance, etc.
  • Free ration, accommodation, medical facilities, and generous leave policy.
  • Pension after retirement (not applicable to Short Service Commission MNS officers).

  3. 3

9. What Not to Do? üö´

To succeed, it is crucial to avoid common mistakes that lead to disqualification.

Career Growth Path in Special Entries

Commissioned Officers (AMC/MNS)

Start as Lieutenant/Captain and can rise up to the rank of Lieutenant General, heading their respective corps.

Havildar (Sports Quota)

Enrolled as a Havildar and can get promoted to Naib Subedar and Subedar. Outstanding performers can also become Commissioned Officers.

Soldiers (DSC)

Ex-servicemen are re-enrolled as Sepoy or Naik and can rise up through the JCO ranks to become a Subedar Major in the DSC.
