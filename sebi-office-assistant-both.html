5. Job Profile & Responsibilities üìã

The roles of Office Assistant and Driver at SEBI are crucial for the smooth functioning of the organization. Each role has distinct responsibilities.

Office Assistant

You will be responsible for a wide range of administrative and clerical tasks.

  • Handling official files and documents.
  • Typing, data entry, and record keeping.
  • Assisting senior officials with day-to-day work.
  • Managing communication (email, phone calls).

Driver / Support Staff

You will be responsible for transportation and vehicle maintenance.

  • Driving officials to various locations.
  • Maintaining the vehicle's cleanliness and condition.
  • Ensuring compliance with all traffic laws and safety protocols.
  • Providing general support as needed.

Written Test Details

The online written examination is the first stage of selection. It consists of two papers, with objective-type questions designed to test your general and professional knowledge. A minimum score is required to qualify for the next stage.

Skill Test: The Practical Exam

The skill test is conducted to assess your practical abilities. For Office Assistants, this typically involves a typing speed test. For Drivers, it involves a driving test and knowledge of vehicle maintenance.

Personal Interview

Candidates who clear the written and skill tests are called for a personal interview. This is a chance to demonstrate your communication skills, confidence, and suitability for a role in a professional environment.
