1. What is the DGFS-DAE Fellowship? üî¨

The DAE Graduate Fellowship Scheme (DGFS) is a program by the Department of Atomic Energy (DAE) that offers an M.Tech or Ph.D. fellowship to deserving engineering graduates. The scheme aims to recruit top talent by sponsoring their postgraduate education at select IITs, followed by a career as a Scientific Officer at DAE.

5. Written Examination Details üìù

The DAE written exam is a crucial step for candidates who wish to join the fellowship without a high GATE score. It is a computer-based test that evaluates core engineering knowledge.

1. Exam Pattern

The exam is a multiple-choice test covering subjects from your undergraduate engineering curriculum.

  • Negative marking is applicable.
  • Questions are designed to test fundamental concepts.
  • Duration is typically around 2 hours.

2. Key Objectives

The objective of the exam is to screen candidates for the final interview based on their technical skills.

  • Assess in-depth knowledge of core engineering subjects.
  • Evaluate problem-solving and analytical abilities.
  • Shortlist a limited number of candidates for the interview.

6. About the DAE Interview üéôÔ∏è

The final interview is a rigorous process conducted by a panel of senior scientists and engineers. It is a technical interview that focuses on your in-depth knowledge of your core subjects and your research aptitude.

Technical Focus

  • Core engineering subjects.
  • B.E./B.Tech final year project.
  • Application of theoretical concepts.

Research Aptitude

  • Problem-solving approach.
  • Innovative thinking.
  • Motivation for research.

General Skills

  • Communication skills.
  • Clarity of thought.
  • Overall personality.

üí° Interview Tip!

Be honest about your strengths and weaknesses. The panel values clarity and an inquisitive mind more than rote memorization.

GATE Score & Shortlisting

Candidates are primarily shortlisted for the DGFS interview based on their valid GATE score. Only those above a specific cutoff are called for the interview. A separate DAE written exam is an alternative for candidates who may not have a high GATE score.

DAE Written Test

The written test is an online exam conducted by DAE to shortlist candidates who may not have a high GATE score. It covers core engineering subjects and general aptitude. Clearing this exam is a pre-requisite for the final interview.

The DGFS Interview

The final interview is a rigorous and highly technical evaluation. The panel focuses on your deep understanding of your engineering fundamentals and your passion for research and development. Be prepared to discuss your final year project in detail.
