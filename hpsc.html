12. Career Roadmap üöÄ

An HPSC officer's career is a dynamic journey with significant responsibilities and promotion opportunities.

HPSC Posts

1. Haryana Civil Service (Executive Branch)

Highest state administrative service. Roles include SDM or SDO.

2. Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP)

Leads the police force in their area. Crime prevention and law & order.

3. Excise & Taxation Officer (ETO)

Responsible for enforcing excise and taxation rules and collecting revenue.

4. District Food & Supplies Controller (DFSC)

Ensures smooth operation of PDS and supply of essential commodities.

5. Tehsildar (Class A)

Revenue collection at Taluka level and resolving land disputes.

6. Block Development & Panchayat Officer (BDPO)

Implementation of rural development projects and Panchayat coordination.

7. Assistant Employment Officer

Promoting employment opportunities for youth and implementing government programs.

8. Traffic Manager

Responsible for traffic management and operation of state transport services.

Stage 1: Prelims

Screening Test (Objective). Merit is based on Paper-I only.

GS Paper I (100 Marks)

100 Questions | 2 Hours | 1/4 Negative Marking

  • General Science
  • Current Events (National & Intl)
  • History of India & Indian National Movement
  • Indian & World Geography
  • Indian Culture, Polity & Economy
  • Haryana Economy, History & Culture

CSAT Paper II (100 Marks)

100 Questions | 2 Hours | Qualifying (33%)

  • Comprehension
  • Interpersonal skills including communication
  • Logical reasoning & Analytical ability
  • Decision making & Problem solving
  • General mental ability
  • Basic numeracy (Class X level)

Stage 2: Mains

Written Exam (Descriptive). Total 600 Marks.

Paper I: English (100 Marks)

English Essay, Precis, Comprehension, Vocabulary.

Paper II: Hindi (100 Marks)

Translation, Letter Writing, Essay, Grammar (Devanagari Script).

Paper III: General Studies (200 Marks)

History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science, Current Affairs (India & Haryana).

Paper IV: Optional Subject (200 Marks)

One subject chosen from the list of 29 options.

Stage 3: Interview

This is the Final Stage (Personality Test). Lasts approx 20-30 minutes.

  • Marks: 75 Marks.
  • Final Merit: Mains (600) + Interview (75) = 675 Marks.
  • Objective: To evaluate the candidate's mental qualities, administrative ability, and understanding of issues related to Haryana.
  • Key Traits:
    • Mental Alertness
    • Logical Ability
    • Balanced Judgment
    • Communication Skills
    • Leadership
    • Integrity

Career Growth Path

1. HCS (Executive) / DSP

Entry Level. Independent charge of a sub-division. Direct experience in administration/law.

2. Additional Deputy Commissioner / Addl. SP

After 8-10 years. Key decision making role in district administration.

After 15 years. Highest administrative post in a smaller district.
