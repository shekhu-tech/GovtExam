13. Syllabus & Resources üìö

Advanced syllabus breakdown.

Stage 1: NRA CET

First-level screening. Single online exam testing Reasoning, Quant, English, and GA at graduate difficulty. Score valid for 3 years.

Stage 2: Mains Exam

Apply for SSC CGL Tier-II, RRB NTPC CBT-2, or IBPS PO Mains using CET score. Specialized in-depth syllabus set by agencies.

Stage 3: Interview/Final

Descriptive Paper, Group Discussion, or Personal Interview. Assesses personality, communication, and leadership potential.

Target Posts

1. SSC CGL

Roles:

Assistant Section Officer (Ministries), Inspector (Income Tax/Excise), Auditor (CAG). Prestigious Group B posts.

2. RRB NTPC (Graduate)

Roles:

Station Master, Goods Guard, Commercial Apprentice. Operational and management roles in Railways.

3. IBPS PO / RRB Officer

Roles:

Probationary Officer, Scale-I Officer. Banking administration, loan processing, and branch management.

Career Progression

1. Entry Level

Assistant / Probationary Officer (PO).

2. Section Officer / Manager

First promotion. Supervisory responsibilities.

3. Under Secretary / Branch Manager

Mid-career managerial role.

4. Deputy Secretary / Chief Manager

Senior administrative leadership.
