Detailed Process

Paper-I (CBE)

  • Type: Objective (MCQ)
  • Total Questions: 200 (50 per section)
  • Total Marks: 200
  • Duration: 2 Hours
  • Negative Marking: 0.25 Marks

PET / PST

Qualifying Nature.

  • Specific standards for Race, Long Jump, High Jump, Shot Put.
  • Height and Chest measurement checks.

Paper-II (English)

  • Type: Objective (MCQ)
  • Subject: English Language & Comprehension
  • Total Questions: 200
  • Total Marks: 200
  • Duration: 2 Hours
  • Negative Marking: 0.25 Marks
