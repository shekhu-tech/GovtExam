Stage 1: Written Exam

For IMA, INA, AFA

  • English: 100 Marks (2 Hrs)
  • GK: 100 Marks (2 Hrs)
  • Maths: 100 Marks (2 Hrs)
  • Total: 300 Marks

For OTA

  • English: 100 Marks (2 Hrs)
  • GK: 100 Marks (2 Hrs)
  • Total: 200 Marks

Note: 1/3rd Negative Marking applies.

Stage 2: SSB Interview

A 5-Day process testing Personality, Intelligence & OLQs (Officer Like Qualities).

  • 1.Screening (Day 1): OIR Test & PPDT.
  • 2.Psychology (Day 2): TAT, WAT, SRT, SD.
  • 3.GTO (Day 3 & 4): Group Tasks, Obstacles, Command Tasks.
  • 4.Interview: Personal Interview with IO.
  • 5.Conference (Day 5): Final Result.
