13. Career Progression ðŸš€

From Lieutenant to General. A glorious journey of leadership and service.

4 Pillars of CDS

1. Indian Military Academy (IMA), Dehradun

Permanent Commission for Indian Army. Rigorous 18 months training.

Motto: "Valour and Wisdom"

2. Indian Naval Academy (INA), Ezhimala

Permanent Commission for Indian Navy. Technical and Executive branches.

Focus: Naval warfare and engineering

3. Air Force Academy (AFA), Hyderabad

Permanent Commission for Indian Air Force. Flying and Ground duty.

Specialization: Aeronautics and aviation

4. Officers Training Academy (OTA), Chennai

Short Service Commission (10+4 years). For both men and women.

No Maths paper required

Stage 1: Written Exam

Objective Type (MCQ). Negative marking applies.

For IMA, INA, AFA

300 Marks | 6 Hours Total

  • English: 100 Marks (2 Hours)
  • General Knowledge: 100 Marks (2 Hours)
  • Elementary Mathematics: 100 Marks (2 Hours)

For OTA

200 Marks | 4 Hours Total

  • English: 100 Marks (2 Hours)
  • General Knowledge: 100 Marks (2 Hours)

Note: 1/3rd Negative Marking applies for wrong answers.

Stage 2: SSB Interview

A 5-Day rigorous process testing Officer Like Qualities (OLQs).

  • 1.Day 1 - Screening: OIR Test & PPDT (Picture Perception & Discussion Test)
  • 2.Day 2 - Psychology: TAT, WAT, SRT, SD (Thematic Apperception Test, Word Association, Situation Reaction, Self Description)
  • 3.Day 3 & 4 - GTO: Group Tasks, Obstacles, Command Tasks, Lecturette, Group Discussion
  • 4.Day 4 - Interview: Personal Interview with Interviewing Officer
  • 5.Day 5 - Conference: Final recommendation and results

Tip: Be natural, confident and show leadership qualities.

Officer Career Path

1. Lieutenant

On Commissioning after Academy training.

2. Captain

After 2 years of service (Army). Flying Officer in IAF/Sub-Lieutenant in Navy.

3. Major

After 6 years of service. Squadron Leader (IAF)/Lieutenant Commander (Navy).

4. Lieutenant Colonel

After 13 years of service. Wing Commander (IAF)/Commander (Navy).

5. Colonel & Above

Selection based. Colonel -> Brigadier -> Major General -> Lieutenant General -> General (Chief of Army Staff)
