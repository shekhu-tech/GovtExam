Exam Procedure

Stage 1: Prelims

100 Qs, 100 Marks, 60 Mins (20 mins/section). Qualifying.

Stage 2: Mains

200 Qs, 200 Marks, 135 Mins. Sections: Reasoning, Eng, Num, GA, Computer. Merit based.

Stage 3: LPT

Tests proficiency in official/local language. Qualifying but mandatory.

Syllabus Breakdown

Prelims Topics

10th/12th level: Puzzles, DI, Arithmetic, Reading Comprehension, Grammar.

Mains Topics

GA: Current Affairs (6 months), Banking Awareness, Static GK.

Computer: Basics, Hardware, Software, MS Office, Internet, Networking.
