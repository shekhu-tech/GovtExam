8. Exam Day Rules & Regulations üìã

Following the rules on the exam day ensures a smooth and stress-free experience.

  • Admit Card & ID: It is mandatory to carry your admit card and a valid photo ID. Without them, entry is not permitted.
  • Prohibited Items: Mobile phones, smartwatches, and any electronic devices are strictly prohibited inside the exam hall.
  • Time Management: Utilize the provided rough paper and manage your time to attempt all questions.

9. Marks and Grading System üíØ

The Telangana Board follows a traditional marking and grading system.

üìù

Marking System

  • Intermediate (11, 12): Each subject is out of a total of 100 marks, which may include practicals and theory.
  • Theory: Usually 60 marks.
  • Practicals: 40 marks (for science subjects).
üéì

Grading System

  • A1: 91-100%
  • A2: 81-90%
  • B1: 71-80%
  • B2: 61-70%
