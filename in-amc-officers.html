Eligibility for AMC Specialist entry is specific to medical qualifications and experience:

  • üéì
    Education: Must have an MBBS degree and a Post-Graduate Degree/Diploma (MD/MS/MCH/DNB/DLO etc.) in the concerned specialty. Must be registered with a State Medical Council.
  • üéÇ
    Age: The maximum age limit is **45 years**, as specified in the official notification.
  • üáÆüá≥
    Nationality: Must be an Indian citizen. Both men and women can apply.

Medical Standards & Attempts üìä

Candidates must be medically fit as per military standards, which are evaluated by a military medical board.

  • SHAPE-1 Standard: Candidates must meet the highest medical standard, known as SHAPE-1.
  • Visual & Hearing: Must meet stringent standards for vision and hearing.
  • Overall Health: Free from any chronic disease or disability that could impact service in varied terrains.

Number of Attempts:

There is no limit on the number of attempts as long as you meet the age and qualification criteria for a specific notification.

5. Training at AMC Centre & College ‚öì

Selected doctors undergo a Medical Officers Basic Course (MOBC) at the AMC Centre & College, Lucknow. This course orients them to the military way of life and their role as medical officers.

1. The Curriculum

Training includes a mix of military and medical subjects.

  • Military Organization and Administration.
  • Weapon Training and Physical Fitness.
  • Combat Medical Support.
  • Military Law and Etiquette.

2. Key Objectives

The goal is to integrate a civilian doctor into a military officer, capable of serving effectively in any environment.

  • Instill discipline and military bearing.
  • Develop physical and mental robustness.
  • Impart knowledge of military medical procedures.
  • Foster leadership and officer-like qualities.

8. Your Preparation Strategy: Smart Work üìà

Success in the AMC interview depends on showcasing your professional excellence and your commitment to serving the nation.

üìö Key Steps:

  1. Revise Your Specialty

    Thoroughly revise the fundamental and advanced concepts of your medical specialty. Be prepared for in-depth clinical questions.

  2. Stay Medically Updated

    Keep abreast of the latest medical research, new treatments, and technological advancements in your field.

  3. Understand the Armed Forces

    Read about the structure of the armed forces, the role of the AMC, and the life of a military officer. Show genuine interest.

üîÑ Habits & Practice:

  • Improve Communication: Practice explaining complex medical topics in a simple, clear, and confident manner.
  • General Awareness: Be aware of major national and international events, particularly those related to health and defence.
  • Maintain Fitness: While the physical standards are different from combat arms, a good level of fitness is expected and essential.

9. Recruitment Cycle & Process üóìÔ∏è

The AMC Specialist recruitment is not on a fixed half-yearly cycle. Notifications are published based on the specific requirements of the armed forces.

Notification Release

Timeline: Released on the official Army website and in leading newspapers as per vacancies.

Application & Shortlisting

Timeline: Candidates apply online, and applications are shortlisted based on eligibility criteria.

Interview & Medicals

Timeline: Shortlisted candidates are called for an interview, followed by a medical examination for those who clear it.

Final Merit List & Joining

Timeline: A final merit list is published. Selected candidates receive joining instructions for their training.
