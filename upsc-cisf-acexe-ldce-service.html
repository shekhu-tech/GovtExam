Industrial Security

As an AC, you will manage security for critical infrastructure like **Airports, Seaports, Power Plants, Space Installations, and Metro Rail**.

Career Leap

LDCE offers a unique opportunity to jump from Non-Gazetted (SI/Insp) to **Group 'A' Gazetted Officer (AC)**, skipping years of normal promotion time.
