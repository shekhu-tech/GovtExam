Complete HSSC Info 🧠

Everything you need to know about the HSSC CET Group D recruitment process.

1. What is CET Group D?

Common Eligibility Test (CET) for Group D is a single-window exam for recruitment to massive posts like Peon, Beldar, Animal Attendant, etc.

2. Selection Process

Written Exam
95 Marks
Socio-Econ
5 Marks
Final Merit
100 Marks

3. Eligibility Criteria

Minimum 10th pass is required. Age limit is 18-42 years. Knowledge of Hindi/Sanskrit up to Matric is mandatory.

4. Exact Exam Pattern

100 Questions, 95 Marks total (0.95 marks per question). Duration is 1 hour 45 minutes.

5. Socio-Economic 5 Marks

The most crucial deciding factor in HSSC. 5 extra marks are awarded based on family income, govt job status, etc.

6. Post Preferences Portal

After the exam, HSSC opens a portal to fill your preferred job roles. Choosing wrongly can land you a tough field job.

7. Document Verification (DV)

Many candidates are rejected at the final stage due to wrong Parivar Pehchan Patra (PPP) data or missing certificates.

8. Career Growth & Promos

A Group D job is not the end. Understand how you can get promoted to Group C (Clerk) via departmental exams.
