Written Test Details

This is an objective-type test with around 80 questions to be answered in 90 minutes. It covers the entire syllabus of your engineering discipline. There is a negative marking of -1 for each wrong answer.

Personal Interview

Shortlisted candidates (typically in a 1:5 ratio of vacancies) are called for the interview. It is a purely technical interview where a panel of experts assesses your fundamental knowledge and problem-solving abilities.

Final Selection

The final merit list is prepared with 50% weightage to the written test score and 50% to the interview score. The selection process is highly competitive, and only top performers make it to the final list.
