Section Officer (SO)

A pivotal role in the Central Secretariat. SOs manage sections, handle files, draft policies, and ensure smooth administration within Ministries.

Stenographer (Group B)

Key to assisting senior officers. Responsibilities include taking dictation, handling confidential documents, and managing the officer's schedule.
