Stage 1: Preliminary Test

This is a one-hour screening test with 50 multiple-choice questions on Mathematics, Science, and General Awareness. It is qualifying in nature; marks are not considered for the final merit list.

Stage 2: Advanced Test

Candidates who clear Stage 1 appear for this test. It consists of 50 multiple-choice questions from the respective trade/discipline syllabus. The final merit list is prepared based ONLY on the scores of this test.

Stage 3: Skill Test

This is a practical, hands-on test to evaluate the candidate's proficiency in their trade. It is purely qualifying (Go/No-Go basis). Candidates must clear this stage to be considered for a position.
