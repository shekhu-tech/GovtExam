5. Physical Endurance Test (PET) üèÉ

The PET is a mandatory qualifying test for all candidates. It assesses your physical fitness and is a crucial step in the selection process. The test standards vary for male and female candidates.

For Male Candidates

  • Running: 1000 meters in 5 minutes 30 seconds.
  • Long Jump: 3.50 meters (3 attempts).
  • High Jump: 1.00 meter (3 attempts).

For Female Candidates

  • Running: 800 meters in 4 minutes 30 seconds.
  • Long Jump: 2.50 meters (3 attempts).
  • High Jump: 0.75 meters (3 attempts).

6. Post-wise Details & Duties üåü

Understanding your role is crucial. Each post has a distinct set of responsibilities within the Food Corporation of India.

FCI Watchman

  • Guarding and securing FCI premises.

Other Group D / Support Staff

Written Exam Details

This is a common objective-type exam for all posts. It acts as a screening test. The paper consists of 120 questions from four sections to be completed in 90 minutes. There is a negative marking of 0.25 for each wrong answer.

Physical Endurance Test (PET)

The PET is a mandatory qualifying test for all candidates. It assesses your physical fitness through a series of tasks, including running, long jump, and high jump. The test standards vary for male and female candidates.

Document Verification

Candidates who qualify in both the written exam and PET are called for document verification. This is a crucial step where your eligibility, as claimed in the application, is verified with original documents and a final medical check.
