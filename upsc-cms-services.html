Central Health Service (CHS)

This is the largest recruiting service through CMS. Under CHS, medical officers are appointed in major central government hospitals like **Safdarjung, RML, and Lady Hardinge Medical College**. They also work in dispensaries under the Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS).

Example: A CHS doctor might treat patients in the emergency department of Safdarjung Hospital in Delhi.

Indian Railway Medical Service

Doctors get the opportunity to work within the vast network of Indian Railways. They are posted in **railway hospitals, divisional hospitals, and health units** specifically created for railway employees and their families.

Example: A railway doctor might work in a railway hospital at a major junction, also responsible for providing emergency medical care during train accidents.

New Delhi Municipal Council (NDMC)

Medical officers under NDMC are appointed in hospitals, polyclinics, and dispensaries located in the **New Delhi area**. They provide primary and secondary healthcare services to the residents of the area.

Example: An NDMC doctor could work in a polyclinic near Palika Kendra, providing OPD services to local residents.

Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD)

MCD operates numerous hospitals and dispensaries in various zones of Delhi. Doctors selected through CMS are posted in these institutions and provide healthcare services to a large population of Delhi.

Example: An MCD doctor might work in a corporation hospital in East Delhi, providing affordable and accessible medical care to the local community.

Paper I Syllabus

A. General Medicine

Cardiology, Respiratory diseases, Gastro-intestinal, Neurology, Dermatology, Psychiatry, Emergencies in General Medicine etc.

B. Paediatrics

Common childhood emergencies, Neonatal care, Growth & development, Nutrition, Immunization, Infectious diseases etc.

Paper II Syllabus

A. Surgery

General Surgery, Urological Surgery, Neurosurgery, Orthopedics, Ophthalmology, ENT etc.

B. Gynaecology & Obstetrics

Antenatal care, Labour, Postnatal care, Family planning, Infertility, Gynaecological cancers.

C. PSM

Epidemiology, Biostatistics, Nutrition, Environmental Health, Health Programs, Immunization.
