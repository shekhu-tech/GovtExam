Paper I (Reasoning, Eng, GA) + Paper II (Professional Knowledge - Obj/Desc).

Detailed Eligibility

Rajbhasha (AM)

Master's in Hindi/English + Translation specialization. Bachelor's with Hindi/Eng subject.

Security (AM)

Min 5 years commissioned service in Army/Navy/Air Force or Police/Paramilitary (Captain rank).

Paper II: Grammar, Translation (Banking terms), Official Language Policy, Letter Writing, Essay.

Protocol & Security
