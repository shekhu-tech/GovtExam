Screening Details

Candidates are shortlisted for interviews based on their performance in a written test conducted by BARC, or through their scores in national exams like UGC-CSIR NET (JRF), JEST, etc. The specific requirements are detailed in the advertisement.

Selection Interview

The interview is the final and only selection criterion. A panel of experts will assess the candidate's grasp of fundamental concepts, research aptitude, and analytical skills. It is a comprehensive academic evaluation.

Final Selection

The final list of selected fellows is based solely on performance in the interview. Candidates who are selected join as Junior Research Fellows and are eligible to register for a Ph.D. program.
