11. Career Roadmap üöÄ

Start as an MTS and rise to Assistant level.

Job Profiles

1. Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS)

General cleaning, carrying files, photocopying, and non-clerical support tasks. Ensures smooth office functioning.

2. Havaldar

Found in CBIC & CBN. Involves security, watch & ward duties. Requires physical fitness.

3. Other Support Posts

Peon, Daftary, Jamadar, Junior Gestetner Operator. All provide support services.

Exam Details

Paper-I (CBE)

  • ‚Ä¢ Session 1: Maths & Reasoning (40 Qs, 120 Marks). Qualifying. No Negative Marking.
  • ‚Ä¢ Session 2: GA & English (50 Qs, 150 Marks). For Merit. Negative Marking (1 mark).
  • ‚Ä¢ Duration: 45 mins each session.

PET / PST (Havaldar Only)

  • ‚Ä¢ Walking: Male (1600m in 15 mins), Female (1km in 20 mins).
  • ‚Ä¢ Physical Standard: Height & Chest requirements apply.

Career Progression

1. MTS (Level 1)

Entry Level. Ensure smooth office functioning.
