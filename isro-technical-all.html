Stage 1: Written Test

This is the first stage, consisting of an objective-type Computer Based Test (CBT). The curriculum is based on the specific discipline/trade. Candidates are shortlisted for the Skill Test based on their performance in this written exam.

Stage 2: Skill Test

Shortlisted candidates are called for a Skill Test, which is purely qualifying in nature. This test is designed to assess the practical knowledge and skills of the candidate in their relevant trade. Candidates must pass this test to be considered for final selection.

Final Selection & Verification

The final merit list is prepared based solely on the marks obtained in the Written Test, from among those who have qualified in the Skill Test. Selected candidates are then called for document verification and pre-employment medical checks.
