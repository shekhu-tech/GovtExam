SSB Procedure

Stage 1: Screening

If you fail here, you are sent back on Day 1.

  • OIR Test: Verbal & Non-verbal reasoning.
  • PPDT: Picture Perception & Discussion Test. Write a story on a hazy picture and discuss.

Stage 2: Psychology

Written tests to analyze your subconscious.

  • TAT: Thematic Apperception Test (12 Pictures).
  • WAT: Word Association Test (60 Words).
  • SRT: Situation Reaction Test (60 Situations).
  • SD: Self Description.

Stage 3: GTO (Ground Tasks)

Group activities to test team spirit and leadership.

  • GD (Group Discussion)
  • PGT (Progressive Group Task)
  • Snake Race / Command Task
  • IO (Individual Obstacles)
