11. Career Roadmap üöÄ

Start as a Constable and rise to Inspector level.

Force Profiles

1. BSF (Border Security Force)

Guards international borders, prevents trans-border crimes, and maintains peace.

2. CISF (Central Industrial Security Force)

Provides security to industrial undertakings, airports, seaports, and nuclear installations.

3. CRPF (Central Reserve Police Force)

Maintains law and order in states and conducts anti-insurgency operations.

4. SSB (Sashastra Seema Bal)

Guards borders with Nepal and Bhutan, promoting security in border areas.

Career Progression

1. Constable (GD)

Initial post. Responsible for security, patrolling, and general duties.

2. Head Constable

Promoted after ~8-10 years. Leads a small squad.

3. Assistant Sub-Inspector (ASI)

Next promotion. First step as an investigating officer.

4. Inspector

In charge of a company or police station.
