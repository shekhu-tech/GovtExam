Indian Economic Service (IES)

IES is a specialized service involved in economic analysis, policy formulation, and implementation. IES officers work in the Ministry of Finance, NITI Aayog, Ministry of Commerce, and other economic departments.

Example: An IES officer might help in preparing the Union Budget, analyze inflation trends, or negotiate international trade agreements.

Indian Statistical Service (ISS)

ISS officers are responsible for the country's official statistical system. They work on the design of large-scale surveys, data collection, analysis, and dissemination. They primarily work in the National Statistical Office (NSO).

Example: An ISS officer might design a survey for the NSSO to estimate unemployment rates or calculate GDP figures.
