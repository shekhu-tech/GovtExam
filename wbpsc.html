5. Mains Structure üìù

Total 6 Compulsory Papers + 1 Optional (for Group A & B). Total 1600 Marks.

Language Papers (400 Marks)

Paper I (Bengali/Hindi/Urdu etc) + Paper II (English). 200 each.

GS Papers III-VI (800 Marks)

History/Geo, Science/Tech, Polity/Eco, Math/Reasoning. 200 each.

Optional Subject (400 Marks)

Two papers of 200 marks each. (Only for Group A & B Services).

Stage 1: Prelims

Screening Test. Objective Type.

General Studies (200 Marks)

200 Questions | 2.5 Hours | Negative Marking

  • English Composition (25 Marks)
  • General Science (25 Marks)
  • Current Events (25 Marks)
  • History of India (25 Marks)
  • Geography of India (with WB focus) (25 Marks)
  • Indian Polity & Economy (25 Marks)
  • Indian National Movement (25 Marks)
  • General Mental Ability (25 Marks)

Stage 2: Mains

Descriptive & Objective mix. Total 1600 Marks (Group A/B).

Paper I: Language (200 Marks)

Bengali/Hindi/Urdu/Nepali/Santali. Descriptive.

Paper II: English (200 Marks)

Essay, Drafting, Precis, Translation. Descriptive.

Paper III: GS I (200 Marks)

History & Geography. MCQ.

Paper IV: GS II (200 Marks)

Science, Tech, Environment, GK, Current Affairs. MCQ.

Paper V: GS III (200 Marks)

Constitution & Economy. MCQ.

Paper VI: Arithmetic & Reasoning (200 Marks)

Maths & Logical Reasoning. MCQ.

Optional Subject (400 Marks)

Two Papers. Descriptive. Only for Group A & B.

Stage 3: Interview

Personality Test.

  • Group A & B: 200 Marks.
  • Group C: 150 Marks.
  • Group D: 100 Marks.
  • Focus: Mental alertness, leadership, clear expression, and West Bengal awareness.
  • Location: WBPSC Office, Kolkata.
