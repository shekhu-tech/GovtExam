Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP)

Highly responsible post. Assists main Loco Pilot in safe train operation. Duties include monitoring signals, checking locomotive instruments, and track monitoring. Requires high alertness and vision standards.

Technician

Backbone of technical departments (Electrical, Mechanical, S&T). Responsible for maintenance, repair, and installation of railway equipment. Requires expertise in specific trades like Fitter, Electrician, etc.

CBT-1 (Screening)

  • Questions: 75
  • Marks: 75
  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Negative Marking: 1/3rd Mark

CBT-2 (Two Parts)

  • Part A: 100 Qs, 90 Mins. Merit decided here.
  • Part B: 75 Qs, 60 Mins. Qualifying Nature (Trade Subject).
  • Negative Marking: 1/3rd Mark in both parts.

CBAT (Psychometric)

Only for ALP candidates. Assesses reaction time, concentration, and memory. Final Merit = 70% CBT-2 (Part A) + 30% CBAT.
