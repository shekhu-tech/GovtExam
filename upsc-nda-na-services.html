Stage 1: Written Exam

  • ‚Ä¢Paper 1: Mathematics (300 Marks, 2.5 Hrs).
  • ‚Ä¢Paper 2: GAT (600 Marks, 2.5 Hrs).
  • ‚Ä¢Total: 900 Marks.
  • ‚Ä¢Note: Negative marking applies. Offline (OMR) mode.

Stage 2: SSB Interview

5-Day Process (900 Marks).

  • 1.Screening: OIR Test & PPDT.
  • 2.Psychology: TAT, WAT, SRT, SD.
  • 3.GTO: Ground Tasks, Obstacles, Command Tasks.
  • 4.Interview: Personal Interview with President/Deputy.
  • 5.Conference: Final recommendation.

Military Career Path

1. Training (4 Years)

NDA (3 Yrs) + Service Academy (1 Yr). Degree + Military Training.

2. Commissioning

Army: Lieutenant
Navy: Sub-Lieutenant
AF: Flying Officer

3. Senior Officer (6-13 Yrs)

Major / Lt. Commander / Sqn Leader to Lt. Col / Commander / Wing Commander.

4. Apex Leadership (15+ Yrs)

Colonel / Captain / Group Captain and above (Brigadier, General).

Note: NDA cadets often have faster promotions.
