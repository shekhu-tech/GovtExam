Stage 1: Written Exam

Objective Type (MCQ). Negative marking applies.

Total: 900 Marks

  • Paper 1 - Mathematics: 300 Marks (2.5 Hours)
  • Paper 2 - General Ability Test: 600 Marks (2.5 Hours)
  • â€¢ English: 200 Marks
  • â€¢ General Knowledge: 400 Marks (Physics, Chemistry, History, Geography, Current Affairs)

Note: 1/3rd Negative Marking applies for wrong answers. Mathematics has sectional cutoff.

Stage 2: SSB Interview

A 5-Day rigorous process testing Officer Like Qualities (OLQs).

  • 1.Day 1 - Screening: OIR Test & PPDT (Picture Perception & Discussion Test)
  • 2.Day 2 - Psychology: TAT, WAT, SRT, SD (Thematic Apperception Test, Word Association, Situation Reaction, Self Description)
  • 3.Day 3 & 4 - GTO: Group Tasks, Obstacles, Command Tasks, Lecturette, Group Discussion
  • 4.Day 4 - Interview: Personal Interview with Interviewing Officer
  • 5.Day 5 - Conference: Final recommendation and results

Tip: Be natural, confident and show leadership qualities.

NDA Career Path

1. NDA Training (3 Years)

Khadakwasla, Pune. Integrated military and academic training.

2. Service Academy (1 Year)

IMA/INA/AFA. Specialized training in respective service.

3. Commissioning

Army: Lieutenant
Navy: Sub-Lieutenant
AF: Flying Officer

4. Senior Officer (6-13 Yrs)

Captain/Major to Lt. Col. Faster promotions for NDA graduates.

5. Apex Leadership (15+ Yrs)

Colonel, Brigadier, Major General, Lt. General, General (Chief)

Note: NDA cadets often have faster promotions and better career progression.
