Exam Procedure

Stage 1: Phase-I

Objective Type. 200 Marks. 120 Minutes.

  • Qualifying in nature (marks not added to final merit).
  • Sections: GA (80), Reasoning (60), English (30), Quant (30).
  • Sectional timing and cut-offs apply.

Stage 2: Phase-II

Objective + Descriptive. 300 Marks.

  • Paper I (ESI): 50% Obj + 50% Desc (100 Marks).
  • Paper II (English): Descriptive Writing (100 Marks).
  • Paper III (FM): 50% Obj + 50% Desc (100 Marks).

Stage 3: Interview

75 Marks.

Tests personality, communication, and economic perspective.

Syllabus Breakdown

Phase I Syllabus

GA: Current Affairs (6-8 months), Banking Awareness, Schemes, RBI Notifications.

Reasoning: Puzzles, Seating Arrangement, Syllogism, Input-Output.

Quant: DI, Arithmetic, Quadratic Equations.

English: Grammar, RC, Cloze Test.

Phase II Syllabus

Economic & Social Issues (ESI):

Growth, Inflation, Poverty, Globalization, Social Structure, IMF/World Bank, Sustainable Development.

Finance & Management (FM):

Finance: Financial Markets, Risk Management, Derivatives, Corporate Governance, Union Budget.

Management: Leadership, Motivation, Communication, HR Development, Ethics.

English (Descriptive):

1. Grade 'B' (Manager)

Assistant General Manager. Leads specific sections (5-7 years later).

3. Grade 'D' (DGM)

Deputy General Manager. Manages entire departments.
