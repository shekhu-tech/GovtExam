Stage 1: Prelims

Screening Test. Objective Type.

General Studies (150 Marks)

150 Questions | 2 Hours | Negative Marking: 1/3rd

  • History (India & Bihar) - High Weightage
  • Current Affairs (National & Int.) - High Weightage
  • General Science - High Weightage
  • Bihar Special (Geo, Eco, Polity) - High Weightage
  • Geography (India & World)
  • Indian Polity & Economy
  • Mental Ability (Maths - 10 Qs)

Stage 2: Mains

Descriptive. Total 900 Marks.

1. General Hindi (100 Marks)

Qualifying (30%). Grammar, Essay, Syntax.

2. GS Paper I (300 Marks)

Modern History & Culture, Current Events, Statistical Analysis (DI).

3. GS Paper II (300 Marks)

Polity, Economy & Geography, Science & Tech.

4. Essay (300 Marks)

3 Sections. Section 3 is dedicated to Bihar specific topics/proverbs.

5. Optional Subject (100 Marks)

MCQ Based. Qualifying.

Stage 3: Interview

Personality Test (120 Marks).

  • Marks: 120. (Total Merit out of 1020).
  • Duration: 20-30 minutes.
  • Focus: Mental alertness, decision making, communication, and knowledge about Bihar issues.
  • Location: BPSC Office, Bailey Road, Patna.
