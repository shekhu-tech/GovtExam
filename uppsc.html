These marks are added to the final merit list.

2. Essay (150 Marks)

3 Essays (700 words each) from 3 different sections (Literature/Social, Science/Eco, National Events/Disaster).

6. New UP Special Papers üåü

Since 2023, Optional Subjects are removed. Replaced by **GS Paper V & VI (400 Marks Total)** focused entirely on Uttar Pradesh.

GS V (UP Special)

UP History, Culture, Architecture, Polity, Governance, Security.

GS VI (UP Special)

UP Economy, Trade, Budget, Agriculture, Geography, Environment.

Stage 1: Prelims

Screening Test. Objective Type.

Paper I: General Studies (200 Marks)

150 Questions | 2 Hours | Negative Marking: 1/3rd

  • History (India & National Movement)
  • Geography (India & World)
  • Polity & Governance
  • Economy & Social Development
  • Environment & Ecology
  • General Science
  • UP Specific GK
  • Merit is decided by this paper alone.

Paper II: CSAT (200 Marks)

100 Questions | 2 Hours

  • Comprehension & Interpersonal Skills
  • Logical Reasoning & Analytical Ability
  • Decision Making & Problem Solving
  • General Mental Ability
  • Elementary Math (Class X Level)
  • General Hindi & English (Class X Level)
  • Qualifying only (Need 33%).

Stage 2: Mains

Descriptive. Total 1500 Marks.

1. General Hindi (150 Marks)

Descriptive. Marks counted.

2. Essay (150 Marks)

3 Essays to write.

3. GS Paper I (200 Marks)

History, Society, Geography.

4. GS Paper II (200 Marks)

Polity, Governance, IR.

5. GS Paper III (200 Marks)

Economy, Science, Environment, Security.

6. GS Paper IV (200 Marks)

Ethics, Integrity, Aptitude.

7. GS Paper V (UP Special) (200 Marks)

UP History, Culture, Polity.

8. GS Paper VI (UP Special) (200 Marks)

UP Economy, Geography, Environment.

Stage 3: Interview

Personality Test (100 Marks).

  • Marks: 100. (Total Merit out of 1600).
  • Duration: 20-30 minutes.
  • Focus: Mental alertness, decision making, communication, and knowledge about Uttar Pradesh.
  • Location: UPPSC Bhawan, Prayagraj.

PCS Career Progression

1. SDM / Dy. Collector

Independent charge of a Sub-Division. Hands-on admin experience.

2. ADM / CDO (5-7 Years)

Additional District Magistrate or Chief Development Officer. Key district role.

3. District Magistrate (10-12 Years)

DM of a smaller district. The highest administrative post in a district.

4. Secretariat (Joint/Special Secretary)

Policy making roles in Lucknow Secretariat.

5. Promotion to IAS

Induction into Indian Administrative Service after ~15-20 years of service.
