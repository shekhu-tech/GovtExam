Selected candidates are appointed to their posts and undergo a period of on-the-job training at DAE's hospitals and medical centers. The training familiarizes them with the DAE environment and specific protocols.

8. Your Preparation Strategy üìù

Success in BARC's medical recruitment is about a holistic approach, combining strong professional knowledge with an awareness of the DAE's unique environment.

üìö Key Steps:

  1. Review Your Curriculum

    Revisit your college textbooks and notes. Focus on the core subjects and clinical aspects of your field.

  2. Stay Updated

    Read medical journals, news articles, and government health reports. Be aware of the latest advancements in healthcare and policies.

  3. Understand the DAE Context

    Familiarize yourself with the basics of nuclear medicine and radiological safety. This shows your interest in the organization's mission.

üîÑ Habits & Practice:

  • Practice Interviews: Prepare for a viva-voce style interview. Practice explaining complex concepts in a simple, clear manner.
  • Professional Demeanor: Present yourself as a calm, confident, and empathetic professional.
  • Health & Fitness: Ensure you are physically fit to pass the medical examination.
