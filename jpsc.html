Stage 1: Prelims

Screening Test. Objective Type. No Negative Marking.

Paper I: General Studies (200 Marks)

100 Questions | 2 Hours

  • History of India (15 Qs)
  • Geography of India (10 Qs)
  • Indian Polity & Governance (10 Qs)
  • Economic & Sustainable Dev (10 Qs)
  • Science & Tech (15 Qs)
  • Jharkhand Specific (10 Qs)
  • National/Intl Current Events (15 Qs)
  • Misc (Human Rights, Urbanization etc) (15 Qs)

Paper II: Jharkhand GS (200 Marks)

100 Questions | 2 Hours

  • History of Jharkhand (Tribal Governance).
  • Jharkhand Movement.
  • Specific Identity of Jharkhand.
  • Literature, Dance, Music.
  • CNT & SPT Acts (12 Qs - Very Imp).
  • Disaster Management in Jharkhand.

Stage 2: Mains

Descriptive. Total 950 Marks (Merit).

Paper I: Hindi & English (100 Marks)

Qualifying (30% required). Marks not added to merit.

Paper II: Lang & Lit (150 Marks)

Choose one language (Hindi, English, Urdu, Bengali, Nagpuri, etc.). Very Scoring.

Paper III: Social Sciences (200 Marks)

History (100) + Geography (100).

Paper IV: Polity & Pub Ad (200 Marks)

Indian Constitution, Polity, Public Administration, Good Governance.

Paper V: Economy (200 Marks)

Indian Economy, Globalization, Sustainable Development.

Paper VI: General Science (200 Marks)

Physical Science, Life Science, Agriculture, Tech, Environment.

Stage 3: Interview

Personality Test (100 Marks).

  • Marks: 100.
  • Focus: Mental alertness, logical ability, balanced judgment, and Jharkhand awareness.
  • Location: JPSC Office, Ranchi.
  • Final Merit: Mains (950) + Interview (100) = 1050 Marks.
