Detailed Syllabus

1. General Knowledge (50 Q)

Current events, Sports, History, Culture, Geography, Indian Economy, General Polity, Indian Constitution, Scientific Research.

2. Reasoning (25 Q)

Analogies, similarities, differences, spatial visualization, visual memory, discrimination, observation, relationship concepts.

3. Numerical Ability (15 Q)

Number Systems, Whole Numbers, Decimals, Fractions, Ratio & Proportion, Averages, Interest, Profit & Loss, Discount, Time & Distance, Time & Work.

4. Computer (10 Q)

Word Processing (MS Word), MS Excel, Communication, Internet, WWW, Web Browsers, URL, HTTP, FTP, Email.

Career Progression

1. Constable

Entry Level. Beat duties and general policing.

2. Head Constable

First promotion. In charge of records or small teams.

3. Assistant Sub-Inspector (ASI)

First Investigating Officer rank.

4. Sub-Inspector (SI)

Chowki In-charge / Main Investigating Officer.
