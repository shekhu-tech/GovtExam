About Crapd.in - Your True Guide

Crapd.in - Your True Guide is an educational app designed to assist users with government jobs, private sector jobs, and AI-based career guidance. This app provides comprehensive information on job opportunities, exam guidelines, video lectures, and multilingual educational notes (in Hindi, English, and Punjabi), helping users in their preparation for various exams and career opportunities.

Our primary goal is to provide users with educational content and career guidance. This app is not affiliated with any government body, and its purpose is solely to provide information about government, private, and AI-based career opportunities.

App Description

Crapd.in - Your True Guide offers users a one-stop solution for preparing for government and private sector exams, as well as guidance for AI and emerging career opportunities. The app covers:

  • Government Jobs Information: Get details on government job exams, eligibility, and application processes.
  • Private Sector Jobs: Access guidance for private sector career options.
  • AI Career Guidance: Learn about career opportunities in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and related fields.
  • Multilingual Notes: Study materials available in Hindi, English, and Punjabi.
  • Video Lectures: Visual content for exam preparation across various fields.
  • Direct Support: Contact support directly through WhatsApp or phone for assistance.

Important Disclaimer

This app is not affiliated with any government agency. While the app provides information related to government jobs, private sector opportunities, and AI careers, it does not represent or is not endorsed by any government organization.

All information is sourced from public domain sources such as government websites and educational content providers. Users are encouraged to verify any information provided by checking the official websites listed below.
