Target Posts

1. Assistant Audit Officer (AAO)

Role:

Highest Grade Pay (4800). Work in CAG. Auditing govt accounts. Only Gazetted post in CGL.

2. Inspector (Income Tax / Excise)

Role:

Field/Desk job. Assessing taxes, conducting raids, preventing smuggling. Uniform post (Excise).

3. Assistant Section Officer (ASO)

Role:

Work in Ministries (MEA, CSS, Railway). Policy making, file work. Delhi-based posting (mostly).

4. Auditor / Accountant

Role:

Work in various ministries like CAG, CGA. Passing bills, checking accounts.

Career Progression

1. Entry Level

Inspector / ASO / Auditor.

2. Superintendent / Section Officer

First promotion (4-8 years). Supervisory role.

3. Under Secretary / Assistant Commissioner

Group A Gazetted rank. Policy level work.

4. Deputy Secretary / Deputy Commissioner

High level administrative power.
