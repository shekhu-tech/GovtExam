Track Maintainer

Crucial role in Engineering dept. Responsible for track condition, safety, inspection, and repairs to ensure safe train movement.

Helper / Assistant

Supportive role in Electrical, Mechanical, and S&T depts. Assisting senior technicians in maintenance, repairs, and installations.

CBT (Screening)

  • Questions: 100
  • Marks: 100
  • Duration: 90 Minutes
  • Negative Marking: 1/3rd Mark

Physical Efficiency Test

Qualifying nature.

  • Male: Run 1000m in 4:15 min & Lift 35kg for 100m.
  • Female: Run 1000m in 5:40 min & Lift 20kg for 100m.

DV & Medical

Candidates clearing PET are called for Document Verification and Medical Exam to check physical fitness for the job.
