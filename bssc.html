12. Career Roadmap üöÄ

Employees selected through BSSC gain significant responsibilities and promotion opportunities.

BSSC Posts

Clerical & Admin

1. Clerks (LDC/UDC)

Administrative work. Responsible for files, correspondence, and data entry in govt offices.

2. Stenographer

Speed writing and typing. Takes dictation from officials and types them.

3. Revenue Clerk (Rajaswa Karamchari)

Maintains land records in rural areas. Vital for revenue administration.

Technical & Other

4. Junior Engineer (JE)

Engineering and technical work (PWD, Irrigation, etc). Oversees and maintains projects.

5. ITI Instructor

Teaching technical skills (welding, fitter, etc.) to students in ITIs.

6. Lab Technician

Health department roles. Analyzing blood/urine samples.

7. Planning Assistant

Collects and analyzes data for government development projects.

8. Driver

Driving government vehicles for officials.

BSSC Career Progression

1. Entry Level

Appointment to Post (e.g., Clerk, Steno). Gain practical experience in department.

2. Senior Level (5-10 Years)

Promotion to Senior Assistant / Section Officer. Taking on more responsibilities.

Administrative Officer. Policy contribution and management.

Based on excellence, promotion to Under Secretary / Deputy Secretary in State Secretariat.
