5. Training & Probation üèõÔ∏è

Newly recruited Assistants are groomed through a specific probation and training period to prepare them for their diverse roles within the corporation.

1. Probation Period

The initial period is crucial for performance evaluation and learning.

  • A probation period of six months from the date of joining.
  • This period can be extended up to one year.
  • Confirmation in service is based on performance during probation.

2. Guarantee Bond

Candidates must commit to a minimum service period with the corporation.

  • Candidates are required to sign a guarantee bond.
  • The commitment is to serve LIC for a minimum period (e.g., 3 years).
  • Leaving before the bond period may involve a financial penalty.

Phase 1: Preliminary Exam

This is a qualifying online exam consisting of three sections: Reasoning, Numerical Ability, and English/Hindi Language. Marks in the Language section are not counted for ranking. It's a test of speed and accuracy to shortlist candidates for the Mains.

Phase 2: Main Exam

The Main exam has five sections: Reasoning & Computer Aptitude, General/Financial Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude, English, and Hindi. The marks obtained in the Main exam are the sole basis for the final merit list.

Phase 3: Language Test & Medicals

After clearing the Mains, candidates must qualify a Language Proficiency Test (LPT) in the official language of the state they applied from. Final selection is confirmed after a pre-recruitment medical examination.
