IT Officer

Manages bank's core banking system, database, servers, and cybersecurity. Requires Engg. Degree in CS/IT/ECE.

Agricultural Field Officer

Promotes rural banking, disperses agri-loans, inspects farms. Requires degree in Agriculture/Horticulture etc.

Law Officer

Handles legal matters, regulatory compliance, and represents bank in suits. Requires LLB and Bar Council enrolment.

HR/Personnel Officer

Manages recruitment, training, promotions, and employee welfare. Requires MBA/PG in HR.

Prelims (Qualifying)

  • IT/Agri/HR/Mktg: English (50 Qs, 25 Marks), Reasoning (50 Qs, 50 Marks), Quant (50 Qs, 50 Marks).
  • Law/Rajbhasha: English (50 Qs, 25 Marks), Reasoning (50 Qs, 50 Marks), GA (50 Qs, 50 Marks).
  • Total Time: 120 Mins.

Mains (Professional)

  • Subject: Professional Knowledge (Stream specific).
  • Questions: 60 Qs.
  • Marks: 60 Marks.
  • Duration: 45 Mins.

Interview

100 Marks. Tests domain knowledge and personality. Weightage 80:20 (Mains : Interview).
