Stage 1: Prelims

Screening Test. Objective Type.

GS Paper I (200 Marks)

100 Questions | 2 Hours | 1/3 Negative Marking

  • History (India & Maharashtra)
  • Geography (India & Maharashtra)
  • Polity & Governance
  • Economy & Social Development
  • General Science
  • Environment
  • Current Affairs (State/National)
  • Merit is based on this paper.

CSAT Paper II (200 Marks)

80 Questions | 2 Hours | 1/4 Negative Marking

  • Comprehension
  • Interpersonal Skills
  • Logical Reasoning & Analytical Ability
  • Decision Making
  • General Mental Ability
  • Qualifying (33% required).

Stage 2: Mains

Descriptive. Total 800 Marks.

Marathi (50 Marks)

Essay, Translation, Precis.

English (50 Marks)

Essay, Translation, Precis.

History, Geography, Agriculture.

GS Paper II (150 Marks)

Indian Constitution, Politics, Law.

GS Paper III (150 Marks)

Human Resource Development (HRD) & Human Rights.

GS Paper IV (150 Marks)

Economy, Planning, Development, Science & Tech.

Stage 3: Interview

Personality Test (100 Marks).

  • Marks: 100. (Total Merit out of 900).
  • Duration: 20-30 minutes.
  • Focus: Mental alertness, logical ability, balanced judgment, and awareness about Maharashtra.
  • Location: MPSC Office, Mumbai.

MPSC Career Progression

1. Tahsildar / DySP / BDO

Entry level. Independent charge of sub-division. Revenue, Law & Order.

2. Deputy Collector (8-10 Years)

Key decision-making role within district administration.

3. Addl. District Collector (15 Years)

Highest administrative post in the district below DM.

4. Joint/Addl. Secretary (State Govt)

Policy making roles in Mantralaya (State Secretariat).

5. Promotion to IAS (20-25 Years)

Induction into Indian Administrative Service based on performance.
